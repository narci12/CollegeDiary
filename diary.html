My Finals Diary

The finals season is a period defined by struggle and perseverance. As the semester comes to a close, the pressure intensifies because of the massive final outputs required of us. In Application Development, we faced the challenge of completing our IoT project within a very tight timeframe. The difficulty wasn't just the technical complexity, but the fact that everything seemed to happen all at once (nagsasabay-sabay). Juggling film production, website development, and art projects simultaneously felt like an impossible task. This finals period has been incredibly demanding, testing our limits as students. However, despite the short deadlines and the heavy workload, we pushed through. We didn't just survive; we delivered. We conquered the struggle, submitted our requirements, and passed everything we needed to pass.
